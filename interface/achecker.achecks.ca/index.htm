When is the AODA compliance deadline?

  • watch_later January 1, 2021 Public websites and web content posted after January 1, 2012 must meet most WCAG 2.0 Level AA criteria

Who does the AODA legislation apply to?

  • business Businesses and non-profit organizations with 50+ employees
  • public Public sector organizations

What is the ADA requirement?

  • gavel The ADA requires websites be accessible WCAG 2.0/2.1 Level AA criteria are consistently upheld as providing an acceptable level of accessibility

Who does the ADA legislation apply to?

  • business Employers with 15+ employees
  • public Also applies to state/local governments, employment agencies, and labor organizations

When is the EQA compliance deadline?

  • watch_later September 23, 2020 Public websites and web content posted before September 23, 2018 must meet WCAG 2.0/2.1 Level AA

Who does the EQA legislation apply to?

  • business The EQA applies to website owners in England, Scotland and Wales
  • public The Public Sector Bodies (Websites and Mobile Applications) (No.2) Accessibility Regulations 2018 applies to public sector websites

How can ACHECKS help?

  • accessibility ACHECKS will monitor and report on the accessibility of your websites, helping you assess compliance and remediate issues
